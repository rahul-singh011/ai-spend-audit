## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no login, no trial period. The audit
is free because SpendLens is a lead generation tool for Credex,
which sells discounted AI credits to startups.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every price is pulled directly from the vendor's official
pricing page and verified with a date stamp. See PRICING_DATA.md
for all sources. AI tool pricing changes frequently — we update
weekly.

**Q: Do you store my data?**
A: Your audit results are stored so you can share the unique
URL. Your email is only collected if you choose to save your
report. We don't sell your data.

**Q: What if I'm already spending optimally?**
A: We'll tell you honestly. SpendLens doesn't manufacture
savings. If your stack is well-optimised, the audit will say
so and you can sign up to be notified when new savings apply.

**Q: What is Credex?**
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor,
Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise and others — sourced from companies
that overforecast their AI usage. SpendLens is their free audit
tool. If your audit shows significant savings, Credex can help
you save even more through discounted credits.
