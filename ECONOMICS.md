# Unit Economics

## What a Converted Lead is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI credits. Assumptions:
- Average deal size: $2,000 in credits per company per year
- Gross margin on credits: ~20% = $400 gross profit per customer
- Average customer lifetime: 2 years
- LTV per customer: $800

These are conservative estimates. Enterprise customers buying
Claude or ChatGPT Enterprise credits could be 10x this.

## CAC at Each Channel

| Channel | Effort | Est. Users | Est. Leads | CAC |
|---------|--------|------------|------------|-----|
| Reddit (r/cursor) | 2 hrs | 200 | 30 | $0 |
| Hacker News Show HN | 3 hrs | 500 | 75 | $0 |
| Twitter thread | 1 hr | 100 | 15 | $0 |
| Credex email list | 30 min | 300 | 60 | $0 |
| LinkedIn outreach | 5 hrs | 50 | 10 | $0 |

All channels are $0 cash CAC. The cost is time, estimated at
~$0 for an early-stage team doing this themselves.

## Conversion Funnel
1000 audit completions
↓ 15% email capture rate
150 leads captured
↓ 10% book a consultation (high-savings cases only)
15 consultations booked
↓ 40% close rate
6 credit purchases
↓ $2000 average deal
$12,000 revenue per 1000 audits

This means each audit completion is worth $12 in expected
revenue to Credex ($12,000 / 1000).

## What Makes This Profitable

The tool costs approximately:
- Supabase: $0 (free tier)
- Groq API: $0 (free tier)
- Resend: $0 (free tier)
- Vercel: $0 (free tier)
- Development time: already spent

At 1000 audits/month the tool is essentially free to run and
generates $12,000 in expected pipeline. At 10,000 audits/month
infrastructure costs might hit $50-100/month against $120,000
in expected pipeline. The unit economics are extremely favorable.

## What Would Have to Be True for $1M ARR in 18 Months

$1M ARR = $83,333/month in revenue
At $2,000 average deal = 42 new customers per month
At 40% close rate = 105 consultations per month
At 10% consultation rate = 1,050 high-savings leads per month
At 15% email capture = 7,000 audit completions per month

So the question is: can SpendLens drive 7,000 audits/month
within 18 months?

**Yes, if:**
1. HN launch drives 2,000 audits in week 1
2. Organic sharing (shareable URLs) drives 30% of subsequent
   traffic
3. Credex email list drives 1,000 audits in month 1
4. SEO on "AI tool pricing comparison" terms kicks in by month 6
5. One viral Twitter thread drives a spike of 5,000+ audits

The $1M ARR scenario is aggressive but not implausible given
the zero CAC distribution channels available to Credex.

## Biggest Risk

Conversion from audit → consultation is the key variable.
If high-savings users don't book consultations, the whole
funnel breaks. The Credex CTA needs to be prominent, specific,
and trustworthy — not a generic "contact us" but a specific
"here's what Credex can save you on top of these numbers."
